This season began on the terrace of an old cafe in the narrow alleys of Paris' 6th arrondissement.
We captured the everyday Parisian life that inspired our new collection.

Moments encountered walking down Saint-Germain in the morning light —
a linen shirt gently swaying in the breeze, the leisure of a cafe au lait,
and the afternoon sun where time flows slowly.

In this lookbook, we introduce silhouettes inspired by the everyday life of Parisiennes.
Styling that is understated, and therefore all the more special.
